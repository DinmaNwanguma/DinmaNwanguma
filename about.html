Our Story

Founded in 1985, Mirian Motors was born from a passion for performance, technology, and bold design. What started in a small garage has grown into a global brand synonymous with innovation and reliability.

We don’t just build cars — we engineer experiences. Every Mirian Motors' model is a testament to precision, power, and the pursuit of perfection. From our designers to our engineers, we’re driven by one thing: redefining the road ahead.
